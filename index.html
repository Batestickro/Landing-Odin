French horn Facts

All wind instruments were originally made of animal horns and french horn is the only one who still keeps a similar sound and form.

The french horn is used in widely in profesional orchestras, it is recommended just calle it "horn" but still is unknown why became known as french horn.

It produce a pure and primitive sound, however is a technological instrument with a complex system of rotary valves and tubes to control pitch and create effects.

There are other types of horns like the Alpine horn from switzerland made of wood with an intresting shape.
